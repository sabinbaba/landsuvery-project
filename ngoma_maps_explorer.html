Project Summary

What This Project Shows

This GIS project demonstrates professional spatial data collection, georeferencing, digitization, and cartographic presentation of RP Ngoma College campus in Eastern Rwanda.

📍
Administrative Hierarchy
Spatial drill-down from Ngoma District → Kibungo Sector → Karenge Cell → College site with connecting arrows.
🏔️
Terrain Analysis
Contour lines at 5m intervals reveal a 45m elevation range across the campus, typical of Rwanda's hilly landscape.
🛰️
Georeferencing
Google Earth imagery aligned to real-world coordinates using 4 ground control points with sub-meter accuracy.
🏗️
Campus Digitization
30+ campus features digitized including workshops, dormitories, libraries, gardens, and infrastructure elements.
🎨
2D & 3D Rendering
Same spatial data presented in both planimetric 2D for analysis and photorealistic 3D for visual communication.
🗺️
Professional Cartography
Complete map layouts with north arrows, scale bars, legends, coordinate grids, and projection metadata.
